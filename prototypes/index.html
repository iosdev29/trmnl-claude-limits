Mascot — 16 frames (4 moods × 4 idle steps)

Pixel-perfect ASCII Claude. Rendered as monospace unicode blocks — each character cell = one source pixel. Mood is driven by the worst live bucket. Idle cycles through 4 sub-frames, one per refresh (~10 min each), so the mascot feels alive over the timescale you actually glance at it. Blink is suppressed on panicked.
